About Eranga Yala Safari

At Eranga Yala Safari, we are passionate about providing exceptional safari experiences. With years of expertise and a deep love for nature, our team is dedicated to showcasing the wonders of Yala National Park. Our mission is to deliver a safe, educational, and thrilling adventure that leaves you with lasting memories.


Why Choose Eranga?

  • Expert Drive: We are knowledgeable and have years of experience and a deep understanding of Yala’s ecosystem.
  • Safety First: We prioritize your safety and comfort, ensuring a secure and enjoyable safari experience.
  • Sustainable Tourism: We are committed to responsible tourism practices that protect and preserve the natural environment.
  • Personalized Service: Each tour is crafted to meet your needs, providing a unique and memorable adventure.
Morning Safari (6 AM - 10 AM)

Start your day with the serenity of the wild on our Yala Morning Safari. From 6 AM to 10 AM, experience the park as it comes alive with the sounds and sights of the early morning. This tour is perfect for early risers and nature enthusiasts who want to witness the park’s wildlife in the soft light of dawn.

Highlights:

Early Bird Sightings: Enjoy the chance to see leopards, elephants, and other wildlife at their most active time.

Cool Morning Breeze: The morning air is cool and refreshing, making for a comfortable safari experience.

Stunning Sunrises: Capture breathtaking photos of the sunrise over Yala’s diverse landscapes.

Afternoon Safari (2 PM - 6 PM)

Experience the magic of the afternoon in Yala National Park with our Afternoon Safari, from 2 PM to 6 PM. As the heat of the day subsides, animals become more active, and the park reveals its afternoon charm. This tour is ideal for those who prefer a relaxed start to their day and want to explore the park in the later hours.

Highlights:

Golden Hour Photography: The afternoon light is perfect for capturing stunning wildlife photos.

Increased Animal Activity: Witness animals as they prepare for the evening, offering excellent viewing opportunities.

Scenic Landscapes: Enjoy the picturesque views as the sun sets over the park, creating a beautiful backdrop for your safari.

Full-Day Safari (6 AM - 6 PM)

Immerse yourself in the full experience of Yala National Park with our Full-Day Safari, from 6 AM to 6 PM. This comprehensive tour offers the best chance to see a wide range of wildlife and explore different areas of the park. It’s perfect for those who want to make the most of their visit and enjoy a thorough exploration of Yala’s diverse habitats.

Highlights:

Extended Game Drives: Spend the entire day exploring the park with multiple game drives, increasing your chances of spotting elusive wildlife.

Diverse Wildlife: From leopards and elephants to birdwatching, enjoy a rich variety of wildlife sightings throughout the day.

Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a delicious picnic lunch amidst the natural beauty of Yala, giving you a break while still surrounded by nature.

Comprehensive Exploration: Cover more ground and visit different sections of the park, each offering unique sights and experiences.

Frequenty Asked Questions

  • What is the entrance fee for Yala National Park?

    The entrance fee for Yala National Park varies based on the type of visitor. As of 2024 Apr., the fees are as follows:

    Foreign Visitors:

    1 Aadult: 13100 rupees
    2 Aadults: 22000 rupees
    3 Adults: 31000 rupees
    4 Aadults: 40000 rupees

    Entrance fees, subject to minor fluctuations based on currency rates, are payable directly at the park entrance when you begin your safari.

  • Are safaris available throughout the year?

    Yes, Yala Safaris operate year-round. The park's landscape changes with the seasons, offering unique experiences, from lush greenery to dry expanses. Be sure to check the weather conditions for the best possible adventure.

  • Is there a pick-up and drop-off service for the Yala Safari?

    Yes, we offer free complimentary pick-up and drop-off service for visitors in the Palatupana, Kirinda, and Tissamaharama areas.

  • What should I wear on a Safari?

    You should wear comfortable clothing that is suitable for the weather. It is also a good idea to wear a hat and sunscreen, as well as insect repellent.

  • What should I bring for a Yala Safari?

    Binoculars (provided), Camera (optional), Sunglasses, Hat, Face mask, Sunscreen, Snacks and drinks, Breakfast (optional), Water Feel free to bring these optional items to enhance your safari experience.

  • Is Yala suitable for all ages?

    Yes, Yala Safari is suitable for all ages. However, it's important to note that some parts of the park may have rough terrain. Families with young children or individuals with mobility concerns should inform us in advance for appropriate arrangements
